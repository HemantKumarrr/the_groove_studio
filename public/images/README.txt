Place your cover images in genre subfolders:

/public/images/covers/bollywood/cover1.jpg
/public/images/covers/bollywood/cover2.jpg
/public/images/covers/rap/cover1.jpg
... etc.

Then update the coverImage field in src/lib/genres.ts with the matching paths.

Recommended image size: 500x500px or 1:1 square ratio, JPEG/WebP format.
